Market Growth in Lagos & Ibadan

Lagos: A Thriving Market

Lagos is experiencing rapid urbanization, attracting investments and driving demand for real estate. With a growing population and limited land, property values continue to appreciate significantly.

  • Annual property value growth: 15-25%
  • High rental demand
  • Infrastructure development ongoing

Ibadan: Expanding Opportunities

Ibadan's growth presents unique investment opportunities as infrastructure development accelerates. With more affordable entry points, Ibadan offers excellent potential for long-term appreciation.

  • Property values increasing by 10-20% annually
  • New road networks opening up areas
  • Growing middle class driving demand
